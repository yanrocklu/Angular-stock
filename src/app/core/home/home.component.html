Welcome to Stock App!


This is an Angular stock web application.

Angular 4 features exhibits in this project:

  • directives, components & data-binding;
  • services & dependency injection;
  • routing;
  • observables;
  • forms (both template-driven and reactive);
  • pipes;
  • HTTP request handling;
  • authentication & route protection;
  • modules & optimization;
  • Firebase & AWS deployment.

Without signing in, you can only view the recipes and shopping list,
After Signing in, you can add/edit/delete the recipes and the shopping list.
